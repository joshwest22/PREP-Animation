Animation

How were those super awesome graph transitions made?

  • Transition (.transition()) was used to carry out the animation. It evaluates every attr() immediately

  • Duration (.duration(miliseconds)) is used to determine the length of time of the animation

  • Easing (.ease()) is used to smoothly accelerate or decelerate through a transition

  • Bar graphs are made by using the d3 core algorithm and then appending a rect object

  • Fun Fact: Tooltips are SUPER easy to use and help discretely display simple information when hovering over different elements of the graph.
